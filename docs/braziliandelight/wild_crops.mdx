**Wild Crops** are Crops that contain their wild and bad cultivated versions. They can be found naturally through the world and can drop seeds or the crop itself when harvested.
You can also pick up them with [Silk Touch](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Silk_Touch) enchanted tools or [Shears](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Shears) to get the decorative block itself.
