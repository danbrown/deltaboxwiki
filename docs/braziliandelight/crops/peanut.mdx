### 📜 Description
Peanut is a nutrient-rich crop introduced in the Brazilian Delight mod. Widely used in Brazilian cuisine, it’s a key ingredient in traditional sweets and festive dishes, especially during Festa Junina celebrations.

### 🌾 How to Obtain
- **Natural Generation:** Peanut can be found growing naturally in **Jungle and Savanna biomes** as a wild plant.
- **Cultivation:**
  - Obtain peanut seeds by harvesting wild peanut plants.
  - Plant the seeds on **farmland**.
  - Wait for the crop to fully grow before harvesting.

### 🍽️ Uses
Peanut can be used in multiple recipes, including:
- [Cajuzinho](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/peanut)
- [Cajuzinho Tray](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/peanut)
- [Peanut Brittle](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/peanut)
- [Powdered Peanut](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/peanut)

### 📌 Trivia
- **Cultural Significance:** Peanuts are widely consumed in Brazil, especially during traditional "Festa Junina" celebrations, where they feature in sweets like pé-de-moleque and paçoca.
- **Health Benefits:** Rich in protein, healthy fats, and antioxidants, peanuts support heart health and help with satiety.
