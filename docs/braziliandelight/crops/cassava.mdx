### 📜 Description
Cassava is a starchy root crop introduced in the **Brazilian Delight** mod. Widely used in Brazilian cuisine, it serves as a versatile ingredient in various traditional dishes. This crop can be cultivated and processed into multiple food items.

### 🌾 How to Obtain
- **Natural Generation:** Cassava can be found naturally in **Savanna biomes**, where it grows as a wild plant.
- **Cultivation:**
  - Obtain cassava root by harvesting wild cassava plants.
  - Plant the root on **farmland**.
  - Wait for the plant to fully grow before harvesting.

### 🛠️ Tools and Interactions
- **Harvesting:** Cassava can be manually harvested or collected using **hoes** for better yield.

### 📌 Trivia
- **Cultural Significance:** Cassava is a fundamental ingredient in Brazilian cuisine, used to make flour, bread, and various other dishes.
- **Nutritional Benefits:** A major source of carbohydrates, cassava is widely consumed for its energy-rich properties.
