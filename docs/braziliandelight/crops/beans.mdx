### 📜 Description
Beans are a staple crop introduced in the **Brazilian Delight** mod. Essential in Brazilian cuisine, they are a fundamental ingredient in various traditional dishes, including the iconic **Feijoada**. This crop can be cultivated and used in multiple recipes.

### 🌾 How to Obtain
- **Natural Generation:** Beans can be found naturally in **Jungle biomes**, growing as wild plants.
- **Cultivation:**
  - Obtain bean seeds by harvesting wild bean plants.
  - Plant the seeds on **farmland**.
  - Wait for the plant to fully grow before harvesting.

### 🍽️ Uses
Beans can be used in multiple recipes, including:
- [Brazilian Dinner](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes)
- [Cooked Black Beans](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes)
- [Cooked Carioca Beans](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes)
- [Feijoada Pot](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes)
- [Green Soup Pot](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes)
- [Tropeiro Beans](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes)

### 🛠️ Tools and Interactions
- **Harvesting:** Beans can be manually harvested or collected using **hoes** for better yield.

### 📌 Trivia
- **Cultural Significance:** Beans are a core component of Brazilian cuisine, often paired with rice in everyday meals.
- **Nutritional Benefits:** High in protein and fiber, beans are a nutritious food source essential in many diets.
