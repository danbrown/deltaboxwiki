### 📜 Description
Guarana is a fruit-bearing crop introduced in the **Brazilian Delight** mod. Known for its energizing properties, it plays an essential role in Brazilian culture and cuisine.

### 🌾 How to Obtain
- **Natural Generation:** Guarana can be found growing naturally in **Jungle biomes** as a wild plant.
- **Cultivation:**
  - Obtain guarana seeds by harvesting wild guarana plants.
  - Plant the seeds on **farmland**.
  - Wait for the crop to fully grow before harvesting.

### 🍽️ Uses
Guarana can be used in multiple recipes, including:
- [Açaí Cream](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/guarana)
- [Açaí Tea with Guarana](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/guarana)
- [Guaraná Juice](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/guarana)
- [Guaraná Powder](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/guarana)
- [Guaraná Soda](https://moddedmc.wiki/en/project/braziliandelight/docs/recipes/guarana)

### 🛠️ Tools and Interactions
- **Harvesting:** Guarana can be manually harvested or collected using **shears** for better yield.
- **Processing:** Guarana berries can be processed into extracts for enhanced effects in recipes.

### 📌 Trivia
- **Cultural Significance:** Guarana is widely used in Brazilian beverages and is known for its high caffeine content.
- **Health Benefits:** Traditionally used as a natural stimulant to boost energy and focus.
