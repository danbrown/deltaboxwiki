Can be found at:

- Forest Biomes

Rarity: Medium

Blocks: Oak Log, Lemon Leaves

Some of the Lemon Leaves are Fertile Lemon Leaves, they cannot be obtained in survival mode. The Fertile Lemon Leaves will grow flowers over time, and generate Lemons.

The player can click with an empty hand on Lemon Tree Leaves Fertile with Lemons to obtain Lemons (1 to 4).
